Project Flowchart

This section illustrates the steps taken to build and train our project. Click each step below to explore details:

Step 1: Data Collection

Data was gathered from various sources, such as databases and APIs, and processed to ensure quality.

Step 2: Data Exploration & Preprocessing

We explored the data to detect patterns and cleaned it by removing missing or erroneous values.

Step 3: Model Selection

Various models were tested, including decision trees and neural networks, to find the optimal approach.

Step 4: Model Training

The best-performing model was trained with the preprocessed data to predict outcomes effectively.

Step 5: Model Evaluation

We assessed the model using metrics like accuracy, precision, and recall to ensure its performance.

Step 6: Model Deployment

The final model was deployed in a live environment, providing predictions in real-time.

Step 7: Monitoring & Maintenance

Post-deployment, we continuously monitor the model's performance and update it as needed.
