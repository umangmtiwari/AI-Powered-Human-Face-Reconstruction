AI-Powered Human Face Reconstruction for Damaged Images

Generative AI has transformed the way we approach image restoration and enhancement. Leveraging deep learning models, specifically Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), we can regenerate damaged facial images, enabling medical, security, and entertainment industries to improve the quality of visuals.

The use of AI in image restoration has led to significant advancements in facial recognition, identity verification, and digital forensics.
